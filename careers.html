Undergraduate/graduate student internships (for our office)

Interested in working in a fast challenging environment? Want to learn more about universities and education? We offer internships throughout the year to current undergraduate and graduate students, regardless of nationality and background experience. Interns will be exposed to the inner-workings of a start-up-like environment while also pursuing their interest in learning more about professional consulting and the college education process. Interns will engage in research projects related to academic counseling, college selection and different cultures. We also encourage a collaborative culture and interns will be supported in implementing their own projects. These projects can range from curriculum development to creating videos on student life on various campuses across Europe and the United States.

Skillset

  • Fluent in Mandarin and English (ideally you have spent a semester abroad).
  • Project Management, able to track processes and changes.
  • Readiness of mind, analytical thinking, rapidly learns new tasks, the ability to act independently and proactively.
